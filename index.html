Organic Garden

Teaching about plants. How to grow them. How to take care of them. How to harvest them. How to process them. Exchange of seeds and seedlings. Identification of nutritional and medical properties. School and university internships. Donations and help in the garden appreciated.

English School

Children: Several days a week, afternoon, English classes in the garden. Learn how to talk, present yourself and interact with foreigners. Adults: English classes to teach highschool graduates to become future tourist guides. English classes for English school teachers.

Workshops

Workshops: Chocolate making. Coffee roasting. Traditional cooking. Identifying and processing of medicinal plants.

Homestay and Retreats

Homestay: stay with a local family, discover the organic garden, eat homecooked meals, take part in Nature School Workshops, discover Tana Toraja off the beaten trail. Retreat: Up to ?? people, stay for up to one week. Every morning different workshop in garden and kitchen. Every afternoon, local driver takes you to most famous sights in Tana Toraja.

Volunteering

International volunteers (also via Workaway): Free accommodation in exchange for twenty hours work a week; working in the garden, teaching English, running social media, setting up crowd-funding, etc. Three meals in exchange for small fee. Local volunteers: Farming and English teaching internships for university students and graduates. Processing workshops in exchange for help in the garden.
