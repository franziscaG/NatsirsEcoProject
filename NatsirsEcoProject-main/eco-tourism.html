Organic Chocolate Workshop

Have you always wanted to find out how to make chocolate from scratch? In our nature garden we grow a large variety of organic cacao trees. We will explain to you how to grow the trees, how to harvest the fruit and how to process them. Depending on the season, you can either try it yourself or we will work with the fruits and beans from the last harvest. The beans have to get roasted, crushed, heated and mashed. Choose your favourite flavour like organic cinnamon, salt or homegrown chilli and add it to your own chocolate. Taste it and wrap it in small bamboo parcels, perfect as a snack or souvenir.

Arrive from Makale in the morning; we can help you organize transport if you don't have any. Roam the garden and discover the beauty of the Torajan Highlands. At lunchtime we provide you with authentic Torajan food, rice, steamed vegetables and fried tempeh or fish. We can easily cater to vegetarians. Ask us for reccommendations abour the area and take off for a short hike or a cultural attraction in the afternoon. Return to your homestay in the evening. You can also book a homestay with us (insert link to homestay section).

This workshop is recommended for children as well as adults.

Torajan Cooking Class

Have you always wanted to take a look into an authentic Torajan kitchen? In our nature garden we grow a large variety of the ingredients for typical Indonesian food. Learn how to harvest peanuts for the delicious Gado Gado or how to grow papayas for a fresh Green Papaya Salad. We will show you how to process all the ingredients for cooking, which ones are our favourite spices and the importance of rice in our culture. We can easily cater to vegetarians. Enjoy your home-cooked meal while listening to Torajan stories and legends.

Arrive from Makale in the morning; we can help you organize transport if you don't have any. Roam the garden and discover the beauty of the Torajan Highlands.
