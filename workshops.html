Our garden is 100% organic and beautifully wild. We love taking care of our trees and plants and our crops are of fantastic quality. It took some time and effort to figure out the best ways to process our fruit and vegetables, and while we're still aiming to improve, we have found unique ways that fit our lifestyle.

Organic Chocolate Workshop - From Bean to 'Bar'

The workshop includes a tour of our beautiful organic garden featuring plants like pineapple, cocoa, cinnamon, vanilla, tumeric, ginger and many more. We will show you how to process your own chocolate from raw cacao bean to delicious treat. Taste your own creation and take some home for later or as sweet souvenir.

Details

  • From 8am to 5pm
  • Snacks and lunch provided (vegetarian and vegan possible)
  • If you want to stay overnight, book a homestay with us

Pricing

  • 500k Rp per person
  • 4 people and more: total price fixed at 1.5M Rp
  • Children < 13 pay half-price
  • Children < 6 are free

Torajan Cooking Class - From Fruit to Food

The workshop includes a tour of our beautiful organic garden featuring plants like papaya, taro, peanuts, lemongrass, tumeric, ginger and many more. We will show you how these ingredients are used in traditional Indonesian cuisine. Under our instruction you will cook your own yummy Green Papaya Salad, Gado-Gado or Perkedel (dishes will depend on availability, with vegan option). The food will be served with our own home-brewed tea.

Details

  • From 8am to 5pm
  • Snacks and drinks provided
  • If you want to stay overnight, book a homestay with us

Pricing

  • 500k Rp per person
  • 4 people and more: total price fixed at 1.5M Rp
  • Children < 13 pay half-price
  • Children < 6 are free
