Our garden is 100% organic and beautifully wild. We love taking care of our trees and plants and our crops are of fantastic quality. It took some time and effort to figure out the best ways to process our fruit and vegetables, and while we're still aiming to improve, we have found unique ways that fit our lifestyle.

Organic Chocolate Workshop - From Bean to 'Bar'

The workshop includes a tour of our beautiful organic garden featuring plants like pineapple, cocoa, cinnamon, vanilla, tumeric, ginger and many more. We will show you how to process your own chocolate from raw cacao bean to delicious chocolate. Taste your own creation and take some home for later or as sweet souvenir.

Duration

We will start in the morning and finish in the afternoon: 08:00-17:00. You will be provided with snacks and lunch (vegan option) and are free to roam the garden or listen to our stories and legends. If you want to stay overnight, feel free to book your homestay with us.

Pricing

The price if 500k Rp per person. For groups of 4 people and more, the total price for the workshop is fixed at 1.5M Rp. Children under the age of 13 pay half-price. Children under the age of 6 are free.

Torajan Cooking Class - From Fruit to Food

The workshop includes a tour of our beautiful organic garden featuring plants like papaya, taro, peanuts, lemongrass, tumeric, ginger and many more. We will show you how these ingredients are used in traditional Indonesian cuisine. Under our instruction you will cook your own yummy Green Papaya Salad, Gado-Gado or Perkedel (dishes will depend on availability, with vegan option). The food will be served with our own home-brewed tea.

Duration

We will start in the late morning and finish in the early afternoon. You will be provided with snacks and drinks and are free to roam the garden or listen to our stories and legends at any point. If you want to stay overnight, feel free to book your homestay with us.

Pricing

The price if 500k Rp per person. For groups of 4 people and more, the total price for the workshop is fixed at 1.5M Rp. Children under the age of 13 pay half-price. Children under the age of 6 are free.

The profits of the workshops directly help to fund our free Eco School.
